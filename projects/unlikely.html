Unlikely Media

With hundreds of millions of total video views, Unlikely Media consisted of a 1M+ follower Instagram network to curate and share viral content. The brand included the accounts @unlikely, @relating, @greattweets, @roomstead, and @questions.

Creating and building these accounts taught me the fundamentals of online virality and social media marketing as the market was growing quickly, allowing me to work with brands such as Goat Case and Fashion Nova. Unlikely Media was acquired in 2019 by a private media company.
