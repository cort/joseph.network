World Places

This project was an online travel brand with the complimentary Twitter account @WorldPlaces. The purpose of this account was to grow an engaging audience interested in travel content, which was submitted by and shared to the account's 350K total followers.

Starting this account was the first time I really felt like I was making a brand that people cared about. I found I learned a lot through simply giving exposure to travel content creators and I even considered releasing merchandise, though the account was sold in 2020.
